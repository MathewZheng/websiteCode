Vulcan Robotics is an award-winning community robotics team from San Mateo, CA. Vulcan was founded in 2014 by 4 students from Aragon High School that wanted to share their passion for robotics amongst other students in the community. Since then, this team has truly come a long way in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), an annual competition where students from around the world build robots to complete in game challenges. Last season we performed well and won 2nd place at the World Championships. This year, we qualified for the NorCal Championship and won the 3rd place Motivate award. Next season, we hope to continue our success in FTC, but we need YOUR help! Make a contribution to our team and help us continue pursuing our passion for robotics.
